About
You’ve made it most of the way through this year’s Christmas list: gift for mom check, gift for dad check, and something for your niece check. But as every year a few days before the big day, you realise that one person your forgot on your Christmas list! Don’t panic, we rounded up the best last-minute DESIGN gifts, that you can make by yourself now to be still in time for Christmas. Real Design-products do not necessarily need ages to produce or consume the budget of your next holiday trip! You can still charm your loved ones with presents that are extremely stylish, useful and qualitative, with help of digital fabrication tools or just with your hands – we all know that handmade gifts mean so much to the recipient!

Brands have always been important for open source projects, and there have been even two projects of quality labels. The Open Hardware and Design Alliance (OHANDA) was a project that aimed at encouraging the sharing of open hardware and designs with a free online service where creators of Open Hardware and Open Design projects could register their products and obtain a common label, similar to a non-registered trademark and based on the four freedoms of Free Software.
